Refund and Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with Cosmacx. We are committed to providing you with high-quality cosmetics that meet your needs. Please read our refund and return policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Online Payments and Refunds

All payments for orders placed on our website are processed online. Once your payment is successfully completed, your order will be confirmed. You may request a refund within 24 hours of your order confirmation. After 24 hours, no refunds will be issued.

2. Refund Request Process

To request a refund within the 24-hour window, please contact our customer service team at macxyy12@gmail.com with your order details. Once we receive your request, we will process your refund and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Due to the nature of cosmetics and for hygiene reasons, the following items are not eligible for refund or return:

  • Opened or used cosmetic products.
  • Items that are not in their original condition and packaging.
  • Products that have been tampered with or altered in any way.

4. Returns

If you receive a damaged or defective cosmetic product, please do not contact us because all our products are for skin types are checked before packing so we will not be able to be able to help you for any issues.

5. Shipping and Delivery

We strive to ensure that your order is delivered within 30 days from the date of purchase. You will receive a notification once your order has been shipped, including an estimated delivery date. If you do not receive your order within the 30-day period, please contact us immediately.

6. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund after we’ve approved it, please check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, as it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. If you’ve done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at macxyy12@gmail.com.
